Spencer Killen

A PhD. candidate at University of Alberta, Canada . Supervised by Jia-Huai You, PhD . My research is in symbolic artificial intelligence. Within the field of knowledge representation and reasoning, I have an interest in:
  • fixpoint semantics (having made various contributions to approximation fixpoint theory),
  • answer set programming (with a strong focus on disjunctive rules), and
  • hybrid logic programming semantics including hybrid MKNF and DL (description logic) programs.
